About Ndala Motors

Ndala Motors is a trusted car dealership based in Botswana, specializing in new and pre-owned vehicles. Our mission is to provide reliable, high-quality vehicles with exceptional customer service to individuals and businesses throughout the region.

Since our founding in 2010, we have grown to become one of Botswana’s leading dealerships with a reputation for integrity and professionalism.

Our Mission

To deliver outstanding vehicle solutions, finance options, and service support that meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of experts is passionate about cars and committed to helping you find the perfect vehicle or part to suit your lifestyle and needs.
